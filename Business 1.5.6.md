## Small Firms
<span style="color:rgb(0, 176, 240)">A</span> <span style="color:rgb(255, 0, 0)">small firm</span> is an independently owned and operated enterprise that is limited in size and in revenue depending on the industry. It requires relatively less financial capital, less workforce and less or no machinery. These businesses are ideally suited to operate on a small scale to serve a local community and to provide profits to the owners.
