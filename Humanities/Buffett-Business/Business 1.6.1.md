## Globalization $\Leftrightarrow$ MNCs

- **Definition:** Globalization is a process of interaction and integration among the people, companies, and governments of different nations, a process driven by international trade and investment and aided by information technology (definition by: www.globalization101.org)
## MNCs/multinational corporations

- **Logos:** Allianz, Verizon, Honda, Samsung, Alphabet, Amazon, VW, Toyota, Mitsubishi, bp, Shell, Costco, Foxconn, Multinational Companies, ExxonMobil, Daimler, Glencore, Microsoft, Walmart+, China Railway, Chevron, AXA, Berkshire Hathaway, Total, AT&T
- **Definition:** MNCs are large businesses which have their operations, factories or assembly plants in more than one country. Examples include Starbucks, IKEA, Toyota, Adidas etc.
- **Handwritten Note (on board):** FD (Foreign Direct Investment)
