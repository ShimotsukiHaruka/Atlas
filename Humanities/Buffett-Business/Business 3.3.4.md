## Revenue
+ Revenue Is The Total Income A Firm Earns From The Sale Of Its Goods And Services.
+ The More The Sales, The More The Revenue.
## Total Revenue Formula
**Total Revenue = Number of Units Sold ร Price Per Unit**

$$
TR = Q \times P
$$
**Where:**
- \( TR \) = Total Revenue
- \( Q \) = Quantity Sold
- \( P \) = Price Per Unit

## Average Revenue Formula
**Average Revenue = Total Revenue รท Number of Units Sold**

$$
AR = \frac{TR}{Q} = \frac{Q \times P}{Q} = P
$$
**Where:**
- \( AR \) = Average Revenue
### Key Takeaway:
Average Revenue equals the Price Per Unit (AR = P).
